Morning: Visit to Blue Grotto

Start your final day in Malta with a visit to the Blue Grotto, one of the island’s most famous natural landmarks. Take a boat trip to explore the stunning sea caves and admire the crystal-clear waters. The colors of the Blue Grotto are mesmerizing and offer fantastic photo opportunities.

Afternoon: Saying Goodbye to Malta

As your trip comes to an end, spend your afternoon saying goodbye to Malta. Whether it’s taking a final walk along the beach, visiting your favorite café, or simply soaking in the last views of the beautiful island, this is a perfect time to reflect on the memories made during your stay.

Start the Day in Naxxar – Breakfast and Relaxation

Begin your day with a peaceful morning in the charming town of Naxxar. Known for its scenic streets and historic buildings, Naxxar offers a wonderful spot to enjoy a traditional Maltese breakfast. Whether you choose a local cafe or bakery, enjoy delicious pastries, a coffee, and a light meal to energize you for the day ahead.

After breakfast, take a stroll around the town to enjoy its peaceful atmosphere. Visit the Naxxar Parish Church and explore the beautiful surroundings, perfect for a relaxing start to your day.

Explore the Mosta Church

After a relaxing morning in Naxxar, head to Mosta to see one of Malta’s most iconic landmarks – the Mosta Dome. This majestic church is famous for its massive rotunda and stunning architecture. It is an engineering marvel, with one of the largest unsupported domes in Europe.

Take time to admire the interior and learn about the history of the church, which was miraculously saved during World War II. The Mosta Church offers a peaceful and awe-inspiring experience.

Relax at the Waterfront

As the day winds down, make your way to the beautiful waterfront to enjoy the stunning views. This area offers panoramic views of the Mediterranean, with the gentle sound of waves lapping at the shore. It’s a perfect spot to unwind and enjoy the evening atmosphere, whether you want to take a peaceful walk or simply relax and enjoy the view.

Dinner at Hi Sushi

For dinner, head to Hi Sushi, a popular spot for fresh sushi and Asian cuisine. Enjoy a wide variety of sushi rolls and other delicious dishes in a cozy atmosphere. Hi Sushi offers an excellent dining experience with top-notch food, perfect for a relaxing evening meal.

End the Night with Fireworks

Finish your day by watching a breathtaking fireworks display, a popular evening event in Malta. Enjoy the spectacular show of colors lighting up the night sky as you reflect on the wonderful day you’ve had exploring the island.

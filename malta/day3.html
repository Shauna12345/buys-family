Morning: Cooking at Home

Start your day by cooking a delicious breakfast or brunch at home. You can prepare traditional Maltese dishes or even experiment with some local ingredients. Fresh bread, cheese, and olives are perfect for a Mediterranean-inspired breakfast. Cooking at home is a great way to enjoy fresh, wholesome meals and start your day on a creative note.

Afternoon in Sliema

After a delicious meal at home, head to Sliema for an afternoon of exploration. Sliema is known for its picturesque seafront, vibrant shops, and cafes. Take a stroll along the promenade, enjoy the views of Valletta across the water, and visit some of the boutique stores and restaurants. Sliema is also great for people-watching and enjoying the lively atmosphere.

Morning Brunch

Start the day with a leisurely brunch at one of Malta’s charming cafes. You can enjoy fresh, locally sourced produce with classic dishes such as traditional Maltese pastries, eggs, and a cup of coffee or fresh juice. It’s the perfect way to energize yourself for the day ahead.

Afternoon in Mdina and Rabat

After brunch, head to the ancient city of Mdina. Known as the "Silent City," Mdina offers a glimpse into Malta’s rich history. Walk through its narrow streets, admire the stunning architecture, and visit the beautiful Mdina Cathedral. Afterward, make your way to Rabat, which is just a short walk away, to explore St. Paul's Catacombs and enjoy the tranquil surroundings.
